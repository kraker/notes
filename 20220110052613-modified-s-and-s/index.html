Warmup#

  • Prying Goblet Squat x5
  • Hip Bridge x5
  • Halo's x5 each direction

Do warmup circuit 3x in this order.

Consider adding cossack squats and/or walking spiderman warmup at some point

Workout#

  • KB Swings 10x10. Either 2-handed or alternating L/R each set.
  • TGU 1x5 L, 1x5 R alternating L/R each set. Total = 10.

Mobility#

Do this barefoot if at all possible, otherwise in flat-soled shoes

Ankle#

  • Half-kneeling dorsiflexion test
  • Band distraction
  • Foam roll
  • Calf stretch
  • Goblet Squat (Optional substitute cossack squat)
  • Half-kneeling dorsiflexion test

Hip#

  • Thomas test
  • Band distraction
  • Foam roll
  • 90/90
  • QL Straddle
  • Half-kneeling hip flexor stretch
  • Thomas test
