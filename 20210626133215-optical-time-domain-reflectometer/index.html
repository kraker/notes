An optical time-domain reflectometer (OTDR) is an optoelectronic instrument used to characterize an optical fiber.

  • The optical equivalent of an electronic Time-domain reflectometer
  • Injects a series of optical pulses into the fiber under test and extracts light that is scattered or reflected back from points along the fiber.
