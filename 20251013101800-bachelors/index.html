Research degree program's:#

Focus on quick associates, but something that also works towards a bachelor's degree that I'm interested in.

Am I interested in furthering my career or just finishing my degree for personal reasons?

It's probably best if I'm able to do online and work at my own pace. If I can avoid needing to attend in-person/online classes on a schedule that fits my learning style better. I'm an outlier in that I'm probably better at self-directed learning more than I am at directed learning.

Computer Science degree courses seem to dovetail the most with my pre-existing interests in science (physics, engineering) and mathematics. I would probably enjoy this more than an Information Technology or Cybersecurity degree based on the course requirements I've seen. It may even lead to a more physics related degree program in Information Theory or similar if I decide to keep going after a Bachelor's...

  • What are my best options for credit transfer?
  • SNHU seems to have pretty good credit transfer

Criteria#

  • No scheduled video lecture requirements. It needs to be self-paced so I can set my own schedule and work it around my job.
  • Self-paced in other respects would be nice. If can cruise through material I already know or that's easy for me... I want to be done quicker.
  • 8 wk semesters (SNHU) is preferable to 12 or 16 wk ones.
  • Maximize transfer credits, life-experience, and certifications? May not know how this shakes out until after my application is processed...
